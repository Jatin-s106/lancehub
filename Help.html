Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on "Join" in the navigation bar, choose whether you're a freelancer or client, fill in your details, and verify your email. You'll be ready to start in minutes!

What fees does LanceFree charge?

LanceFree charges a 10% service fee on all transactions for freelancers. Clients pay a small processing fee of 3% per payment. These fees help us maintain the platform and provide excellent support.

How do I submit a bid on a project?

Browse available projects, click on one that interests you, review the requirements, and click "Submit Proposal". Include your price, timeline, and a detailed explanation of how you'll complete the project.

How does the payment escrow system work?

When a project starts, the client deposits funds into escrow. The freelancer works on the project, and upon completion and client approval, the funds are released. This protects both parties.

What if I have a dispute with a client/freelancer?

If you encounter issues, first try to resolve them through communication. If that doesn't work, you can open a dispute through your contract page. Our support team will review the case and help mediate a fair resolution.

How can I improve my freelancer profile?

Add a professional photo, write a compelling bio, showcase your best work in your portfolio, list all relevant skills, and maintain a high rating by delivering quality work on time. Client reviews are crucial!

Can I work with clients from other countries?

Absolutely! LanceFree is a global platform. You can work with clients from over 180 countries. Payments are processed in USD, and we handle currency conversions automatically.

How long does it take to withdraw my earnings?

Once funds are released from escrow, you can withdraw them immediately. Depending on your payment method (PayPal, bank transfer, etc.), it typically takes 1-5 business days to reach your account.
